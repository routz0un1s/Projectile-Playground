Projectile Playground


------------------------------------------

Projectile Playground is an interactive physics simulation, that allows you to visualize and understand projectile motion.

You can change variables such as gravity, velocity and angle, to see how the distance covered and the maximum height are affected.

------------------------------------------


Projectile motion describes how an object moves through the air under the influence of gravity. The motion can be split into two parts:

 -> Horizontal motion: constant velocity

 -> Vertical motion: acceleration due to gravity.
